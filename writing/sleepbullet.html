Zenith's Journal > 10 Jan 2027

First four-hour sleep tonight. Sceptical, as all Bayesian's ought to be, but also excited…

The science seems sound. Dozens of peer-reviewed papers on medRxiv, and upcoming Phase III trials. Most compelling study: The four-hour sleep night: neuroemumodulation shortens sleep by 50%. Participants (n=963) beta tested the app, which functioned as normal for the randomly assigned experimental group, but for the control group its functionality was disabled (placebo). Fully 97.5% of the experimental group reported feeling the same or better than baseline, and memory and learning were unaffected. Impressive. The control group reported feeling, unsurprisingly, chronically sleep deprived, and memory and learning fell off a precipice.

SleepBullet is sophisticated, utilising recent advances in miniaturisation, power, and shielding of magnetisation. It works in two phases:

  1. The 13-night neuroemulation phase, in which the user's brain activity is measured, mapped, and modelled. SleepBullet uses micro-magnetoencephalography (with a supersensitive magnetometer normally used for navigation) to measure the user's brain activity during sleep and ambient magnetic noise. Using entropy-based signal-extraction techniques, SleepBullet filters the noise, so the brain's tiny magnetic fields sing like cicadas in the quite magnetic night. The brainwaves are fed into SleepMind, a supercomputer that creates a one-to-one model of the user's brain – a virtual copy.
  2. The neuromodulation phase, in which the user's brain is modulated to accelerate sleep cycles. Focused transcranial magnetic stimulation effectively penetrates the skull, enabling neuromodulation, both depolarisation and hyperpolarisation. Spatial-temporal, small population-neuron stimulation precision is achieved using a stereo-superconducting magnet (2 Tesla) constructed from very high-temperature superconducting tape (a micro-Faraday cage provides magnetic shielding to avoid wiping everything on the phone). Then, using the emulation model, modulating magnetic fields are induced in the user's brain that accelerate sleep cycles. In effect, the brain (and body) subjectively experiences the normal five, 90- minute sleep cycles, but in half the time. God turns the time dial from 1 to 2.
